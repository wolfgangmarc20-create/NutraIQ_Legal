30-Day Money-Back Guarantee
We're confident you'll love NutraIQ. If you're not satisfied for any reason, get a full refund within 30 days.

1. Refund Eligibility

You are eligible for a full refund if:

  • You request a refund within 30 days of your purchase date
  • Your account is in good standing (no Terms of Service violations)
  • You provide a valid reason for the refund (helps us improve)

2. How to Request a Refund

Step 1: Email us at [info@nutraiq.net]

  • Subject Line: "Refund Request"
  • Include:
    • Your account email address
    • Reason for refund (optional but appreciated)
    • Purchase date (if you remember it)

Step 2: We review your request (usually within 24-48 hours)

Step 3: Refund approved and processed

3. Refund Processing

  • Approval Time: Usually within 1 business day
  • Processing Time: 5-10 business days
  • Method: Refunded to your original payment method
  • Confirmation: You'll receive an email when processed

4. What Happens After Refund

  • Your subscription is cancelled immediately
  • You lose access to premium features
  • Your account data is retained for 30 days (in case you change your mind)
  • After 30 days, you can request account deletion

5. Subscription Cancellation (Without Refund)

You can cancel your subscription anytime without requesting a refund:

  • How: Go to Account Settings → Billing → Cancel Subscription
  • Effect: No further charges after current period ends
  • Access: You keep access until the end of your paid period
  • Partial Refunds: We don't offer prorated refunds for partial months

6. Exceptions (No Refund)

Refunds are NOT available for:

  • Requests made more than 30 days after purchase
  • Accounts terminated for violating Terms of Service
  • Free trial periods (no payment, no refund needed)
  • Chargebacks (if you file a chargeback, we can't also refund)

7. Beta Version Notice

During our beta period:

  • Your feedback helps us improve the product
  • We may offer alternatives before processing the refund

  • Paddle handles all payment-related customer service

9. Currency & Taxes

  • Currency: Refunded in the same currency as original purchase
  • Taxes: Any VAT or sales tax is also refunded
  • Exchange Rates: Your bank's rates apply

10. Disputes
